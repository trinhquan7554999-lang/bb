About SteelWeigh

Founded in 2015, SteelWeigh has been at the forefront of kitchen precision instruments, designing and crafting premium stainless steel food scales that combine accuracy with elegance.

Our Story

From Kitchen Problem to Innovative Solution

SteelWeigh started out with a simple frustration: why can’t kitchen scales be both super precise and beautiful? Our founders, Emma and Michael Turner, both keen home chefs, constantly found the kitchen scales available to them were either very accurate but ugly, or stylish but inaccurate. In 2015, Kitchenville put together a small team of engineers and designers and started designing the first SteelWeigh scale - the Precision Classic. This initial product was so successful that it validated their intuition that people would pay more for something that actually worked — and looked good while doing it. Today, SteelWeigh has expanded beyond that small workshop and now has over 75 employees, but we are still dedicated to making precision measurement tools that improve both the functionality and appearance of your kitchen. p>

Our Manufacturing Process

From concept to kitchen, here's how we craft our premium scales.

1

Design & Engineering

Our story about product starts with our designers, who work hand in hand with engineers to design scales that are both pretty and functional. Every detail is perfected using 3D modeling before we proceed to prototyping.

2

Material Selection

We use the best quality stainless steel and components available. And all of our materials are tested for structural integrity as well as lasting aesthetics.

3

Precision Manufacturing

Our scales are fitted and manufactured with a high degree of equipment, with very close tolerances. The process is performed independently for each sensor so that all measurement ranges are consistent.

4

Quality Testing

All scales undergo multiple testing parts — verifying weight throughout the entire measuring range, drop testing, and environmental condition testing so you can trust in any kitchen.

5

Final Inspection

Each scale is then inspected individually by our quality assurance team to look for any cosmetic issues and ensure it works. What does the SteelWeigh seal even mean?Only scales that pass our demanding process receive the SteelWeigh seal of approval.

6

Sustainable Packaging

Our scales are packed using recyclable materials designed for protecting the product from damage during shipping, while having the lowest possible impact on the environment. Our packaging is as considered as our products.
