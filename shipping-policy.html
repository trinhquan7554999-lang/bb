Shipping Policy

Last Updated: August 15, 2023

Introduction

This Shipping Policy outlines the shipping methods, delivery timeframes, costs, and related procedures for products purchased from SteelWeigh's website www.steelweigh.com (the "Site"). We are committed to providing reliable and timely delivery of our premium kitchen scales and accessories to our customers worldwide.

Shipping Coverage

SteelWeigh currently ships to the following regions:

  • United States (all 50 states and territories)
  • Canada
  • European Union countries
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • New Zealand
  • Select Asian countries (Japan, Singapore, South Korea, Hong Kong)

If your country is not listed above, please contact our customer service at shipping@steelweigh.com to inquire about shipping options.

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. We strive to ship all orders as quickly as possible, but please note that during peak seasons (holidays, special promotions), processing times may be slightly longer.

If there are any unforeseen delay in processing your order, we will notify you via email.

Shipping Confirmation and Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email that includes a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order status by logging into your SteelWeigh account and viewing the "Order History" section.

Please note that some international shipments may have limited tracking capabilities once they leave the United States and enter the destination country's postal system.

Shipping Address

It is the customer's responsibility to provide a correct and complete shipping address at checkout. SteelWeigh is not responsible for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer.

If you need to change your shipping address after placing an order, please contact our customer service immediately at shipping@steelweigh.com. We can only modify the shipping address if the order has not yet been processed for shipping.

Package Handling and Delivery

SteelWeigh partners with reputable shipping carriers to ensure safe and timely delivery of your products. However, once a package is handed over to the shipping carrier, the delivery timeline is controlled by the carrier.

For domestic shipments, signature confirmation is required for orders over $150. For international shipments, signature confirmation is required for all orders.

If you are not available to sign for your package, the carrier will leave a delivery attempt notice with instructions on how to reschedule delivery or pick up your package from a local facility.

Delivery Delays

Delivery times are estimates and are not guaranteed. Various factors beyond our control may affect delivery times, including:

  • Weather conditions
  • Natural disasters
  • Customs delays (for international shipments)
  • Carrier service disruptions
  • High volume shipping periods (holidays)
  • Incorrect or incomplete address information

SteelWeigh is not responsible for shipping delays caused by these factors.

International Shipping and Customs

For international orders, the recipient is responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and import fees imposed by the destination country. These fees are not included in the purchase price or shipping cost and are collected by the shipping carrier or local customs authority upon delivery.

SteelWeigh has no control over these charges and cannot predict their amount. Customs policies vary widely from country to country; please contact your local customs office for more information.

To avoid potential delays, please ensure that all information provided for international shipping is accurate and complete.

Missing, Damaged, or Lost Packages

If your package is marked as "Delivered" by the carrier but you have not received it, please:

  1. Check with neighbors and around your property
  2. Contact your local post office or carrier facility
  3. Wait 48 hours, as sometimes packages are marked as delivered before they actually arrive

If your package is still missing after these steps, please contact us at shipping@steelweigh.com within 7 days of the delivery date.

For damaged packages, please refuse delivery if possible and contact us immediately. If you've already accepted the package, please take photos of the damaged box and products and contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

For lost packages, we will work with the shipping carrier to locate your package or process a claim. This process typically takes 5-10 business days.

Returns and Shipping

For information about return shipping, please refer to our Refund Policy.

Gift Shipping

We offer gift shipping options at checkout. You can choose to include a personalized gift message and remove price information from the packing slip. Gift wrapping is available for an additional fee of $5.95 per item.

Multiple items in a gift order will be shipped together when possible, unless you specifically request separate shipments at checkout (additional shipping charges will apply).

Environmental Commitment

SteelWeigh is committed to reducing our environmental impact. We use recyclable and biodegradable packaging materials whenever possible and are continuously working to optimize our shipping processes to minimize carbon emissions.

Changes to This Shipping Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Shipping Policy at any time. Changes will become effective when posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to check this page periodically for any changes.
