Standard shipping within the continental United States typically takes 3-5 business days from the time your order ships. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary by location but generally take 7-14 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

All SteelWeigh scales come with a 2-year limited warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. If your scale develops a fault covered by the warranty, we will repair or replace it free of charge. Simply contact our customer service team with your order details and a description of the problem to initiate the warranty process.

Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

Battery life varies by model and usage frequency, but typically our scales can operate for 6-12 months with normal use before requiring new batteries. All our digital scales feature auto-off functionality to conserve battery life and low battery indicators to let you know when it's time for a replacement.
