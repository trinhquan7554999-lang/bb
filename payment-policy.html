Payment Policy

Last Updated: August 15, 2023

Introduction

This Payment Policy outlines the terms and conditions related to payments made to SteelWeigh ("we," "our," or "us") for products purchased through our website www.steelweigh.com (the "Site"). By making a purchase on our Site, you agree to comply with and be bound by this Payment Policy.

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for purchases made on our Site:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
  • Debit Cards: Visa Debit, MasterCard Debit
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Bank Transfers: Available for select regions and order amounts
  • Gift Cards: SteelWeigh-issued gift cards

We reserve the right to add or remove payment methods at our discretion. Any changes to our accepted payment methods will be updated on this page.

Payment Security

We take your payment security seriously and implement various measures to protect your financial information:

  • All payment transactions are encrypted using SSL technology
  • We comply with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) requirements
  • We do not store complete credit card information on our servers
  • We partner with reputable payment processors to handle financial transactions
  • We implement fraud detection and prevention measures

While we implement these security measures, we cannot guarantee that unauthorized access to your information will never occur. We encourage you to maintain the security of your account credentials and notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized use of your payment information.

Pricing and Currency

All prices listed on our Site are in US Dollars (USD) unless otherwise specified. For customers outside the United States, the final amount charged will be converted to your local currency by your payment provider at their current exchange rate, which may include conversion fees.

We endeavor to ensure that all prices listed on our Site are accurate. However, errors may occasionally occur. If we discover an error in the price of products you have ordered, we will inform you as soon as possible and give you the option of reconfirming your order at the correct price or canceling it.

Billing Process

When you place an order, we will charge your selected payment method immediately upon order confirmation. The charge will appear on your statement as "SteelWeigh" or "SW Kitchen Products."

For subscription-based services, you authorize us to automatically charge the payment method on file according to the subscription terms you selected (monthly, quarterly, or annually). You will receive a receipt for each payment via email.

Invoices and Receipts

After completing your purchase, you will receive an order confirmation email containing the details of your purchase and a receipt. You can also access your order history and download invoices from your account dashboard.

If you require a specific format for your invoice or have any questions about your receipt, please contact our customer service team at billing@steelweigh.com.

Payment Failures

If your payment is declined or fails for any reason, we will notify you via email. You can resolve the issue by:

  • Updating your payment information in your account settings
  • Contacting your financial institution to resolve any issues
  • Selecting an alternative payment method

For subscription services, if we are unable to charge your payment method, we may suspend your subscription until the payment issue is resolved.

Taxes

Applicable sales tax will be charged based on your shipping address and the tax regulations of your jurisdiction. The tax amount will be calculated and displayed during the checkout process before you complete your purchase.

For international orders, customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees imposed by their country of residence. These charges are not included in our prices and will be collected by the relevant authority at the time of delivery.

Discounts and Promotions

We may offer promotional codes and discounts from time to time. These offers are subject to the following terms:

  • Promotional codes must be entered at checkout to be applied
  • Discounts cannot be applied retroactively to previous purchases
  • Unless otherwise stated, promotions cannot be combined with other offers
  • We reserve the right to modify or cancel promotions at any time
  • Specific terms and conditions may apply to individual promotions

Refunds

Our refund policy is outlined in our separate Refund Policy document. When a refund is issued, it will be processed using the same payment method used for the original purchase. Depending on your payment provider, it may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Changes to This Payment Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Payment Policy at any time. Any changes will become effective when we post the revised policy on our Site. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our payment policies.
